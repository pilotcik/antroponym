Meet the Animals

Lion

The lion is called the "King of the Jungle".

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals.

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest animals in the world.

Tiger

Tigers have beautiful striped fur.

Panda

Pandas mostly eat bamboo and love to sleep.
