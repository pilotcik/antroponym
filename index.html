What's Anthroponyms

Anthroponyms refer to human names, and this category of names can be broken down into various subtypes, each with its own cultural, historical, and linguistic significance. These names are used to identify and distinguish individuals, and they can carry personal, familial, or societal meaning. Anthroponyms are a fascinating aspect of linguistics, specifically within the study of onomastics (the study of names).
